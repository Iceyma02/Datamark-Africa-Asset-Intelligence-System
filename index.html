The Current Situation
Valuable data. Manual process.
Slow decisions.

Reports built by hand

Brand performance reports are compiled manually in Excel each month — hours of work that could take seconds.

Assets go missing silently

Parts sit in "Pending" for months or years with no alert. No one knows until it's too late to recover.

Clients can't see their own data

Clients wait for a monthly PDF. They can't search, filter, or ask questions about their own fleet in real time.

Brand decisions made slowly

Procurement teams lack the clear brand-vs-brand comparison needed to switch suppliers or make warranty claims.

The Solution
Six modules.
One intelligent platform.

Built on the data DATAMARK already captures — no new hardware, no disruption to operations on the ground.

01
📊
Brand Performance Engine
Compare every component brand by scrap rate, average days on unit, and failure pattern. Make supplier decisions with data, not guesswork.
Core Module
  • Automatic monthly brand ranking
  • New vs In-Use failure comparison
  • Supplier warranty claim support
02
📍
Asset Lifecycle Tracker
A live, searchable view of every marked component — current location, previous location, days on unit, and status. No more phone calls to find a part.
Core Module
  • Search any asset by QR code or ID
  • Full movement history per component
  • Vehicle-level component summary
03
🔔
Exception & Alert Centre
Automatic flags for stuck assets, idle time beyond threshold, excessive replacement, and unauthorised movements — sent to the right manager, immediately.
Core Module
  • Configurable alert thresholds per client
  • Weekly exception digest emails
  • Pending & idle asset detection
04
🧠
Scrap Intelligence
Understand why components fail — not just that they failed. Risk-score active assets approaching historical scrap thresholds before they become a problem.
Premium
  • Failure pattern analysis per brand
  • Risk flag on at-risk active assets
  • Procurement recommendation report

How It Works
Nothing changes on the ground.
Everything changes in the office.
Step 01

DATAMARK continues as normal

Field teams mark and scan parts daily. The QR-coded inspection data flows into DATATRACK exactly as it does today.

Step 02

Data feeds the intelligence layer

The Asset Intelligence System reads DATATRACK data and processes it into live dashboards, alerts, and reports automatically.

Step 03

Your team sees everything

Internal dashboards show brand performance, pending assets, scrap trends — updated daily. No more Excel digging.

Step 04

You deliver better reports

Monthly client reports are generated automatically. Account managers send them in one click instead of building from scratch.
